# 8.2 Bundled Solution Packages

**AI Starter Pack** — ₹3,00,000 / $4,000
- AI Readiness Assessment
- AI Foundations Workshop (half-day, for leadership team)
- 3 no-code workflow automations
- 90-day quick-win roadmap
*Best for:* SMEs beginning their AI journey

**AI Growth Bundle** — ₹12,00,000 / $15,000
- AI Transformation Strategy
- 2 AI agent builds (chatbot + one task agent)
- 1 department-level automation package
- 10-seat AI Foundations course licenses
- 3-month advisory retainer (Starter tier)
*Best for:* Mid-market companies ready to operationalize AI

**Enterprise AI Program** — Custom (typically ₹50,00,000 – ₹2,00,00,000 / $65,000 – $250,000)
- Full AI Transformation Strategy
- CoE Design & Setup
- Multiple automation workstreams
- Custom corporate training program (organization-wide)
- 12-month Strategic Advisory Retainer
- CerebroFlow or CerebroAgent Enterprise license
*Best for:* Enterprises committing to organization-wide AI transformation
