# 8.1 Standard Engagement Structures

**One-Time Projects**
Defined scope, defined deliverables, fixed fee or time-and-materials. Suitable for assessments, strategy engagements, one-time automation builds.

**Retainer Engagements**
Monthly recurring fee for ongoing advisory or managed services. Provides budget predictability for clients and revenue predictability for CerebroHive.

**Milestone-Based Programs**
Large projects (CoE setup, transformation programs) split into milestones with payment tied to delivery. Reduces risk for the client; maintains cash flow for CerebroHive.

**Subscription Products**
CerebroFlow, CerebroAgent, CerebroLearn — monthly or annual SaaS subscriptions. Annual subscriptions at 15–20% discount to monthly equivalent.

**Usage-Based Pricing**
For some automation services (document processing, conversation volume) — priced per unit of work processed rather than fixed monthly.
