# 8.3 Partner & Reseller Programs

**Implementation Partner Program**
For consulting firms, system integrators, and technology companies that want to resell or co-deliver CerebroHive services and products.

- **Referral Partner:** Refer clients, earn 10–15% commission on closed deals. No delivery responsibility.
- **Reseller Partner:** White-label CerebroHive services or products under own brand. Revenue share model.
- **Implementation Partner:** Co-deliver with CerebroHive. Joint GTM, shared delivery, certified by CerebroHive on methodology.
- **Technology Partner:** Integrate CerebroHive products with partner technology platforms. Co-marketing, joint solution bundling.
