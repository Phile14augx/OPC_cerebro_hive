# CerebroHive — Division Overview

## 1. Division Overview

CerebroHive operates four integrated business divisions, each generating independent revenue while feeding the others through shared brand authority, client relationships, and technical IP.

| Division | Core Function | Primary Buyer | Revenue Model |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CerebroHive Consulting** | AI Strategy, Governance & Transformation | C-suite, VP/Directors | Project fees + retainers |
| **CerebroHive Automation** | Workflow Automation, AI Agents, RPA | COOs, Ops Directors, IT Heads | Build fees + maintenance SLAs |
| **CerebroHive Academy** | AI Education, Bootcamps, Certifications | Individuals, L&D Teams, HR | Course sales + corporate contracts |
| **CerebroHive Labs** | Research, OSS, Experimental Products | Builders, Researchers, Partners | Licensing, grants, product spin-offs |

The flywheel logic: Consulting generates trusted client relationships and diagnostic data → Automation turns those insights into deployed systems → Academy builds the talent pipeline to staff and sustain those systems → Labs generates the proprietary IP that powers all four divisions.
