# 2.4 AI Use Case Discovery & Prioritization Workshops

**What it is:** A facilitated 1–3 day workshop series (in-person or virtual) with cross-functional leadership teams to identify, evaluate, and prioritize AI use cases specific to the client's business.

**Workshop Modules:**
- **Module 1 — AI Landscape Briefing:** What AI can and cannot do today; where organizations are succeeding; category-specific case studies for the client's industry
- **Module 2 — Process Archaeology:** Structured walkthrough of operational processes to surface automation and AI candidates
- **Module 3 — Opportunity Scoring:** Each use case scored against a 6-factor matrix (feasibility, data availability, ROI magnitude, strategic alignment, implementation complexity, time-to-value)
- **Module 4 — Sequencing & Roadmapping:** Building the implementation sequence — quick wins, strategic bets, and moonshots
- **Module 5 — Success Metric Definition:** KPIs, baseline measurements, and target outcomes for each prioritized use case

**Output:** A ranked, scored, business-case-ready use case portfolio with implementation sequencing and a 12-month execution plan.

**Format:** 1-day (quick scan), 2-day (standard), or 3-day (deep enterprise) versions available.

**Pricing:** ₹1,50,000 – ₹5,00,000 / $2,000 – $7,000 per workshop engagement
