# 2.2 AI Transformation Strategy

**What it is:** A full strategic engagement that takes the findings from a Readiness Assessment (or equivalent) and builds an AI transformation program — a multi-quarter roadmap with workstreams, resource requirements, build-vs-buy decisions, vendor evaluation criteria, governance frameworks, and financial business cases for each initiative.

**Deliverables:**
- AI Strategic Vision Document (2–3 year horizon)
- Initiative Portfolio with prioritization matrix (effort vs. impact vs. risk)
- Build vs. Buy vs. Partner Decision Framework for each use case
- Technology Architecture Blueprint — AI stack recommendations
- Data Strategy & Infrastructure Roadmap
- AI Governance Framework — policies, oversight structure, ethics guidelines
- Organizational Change Management Plan — training, role evolution, communication strategy
- Financial Business Case — projected ROI, cost model, and payback period for top 5 initiatives
- Vendor Shortlist & Evaluation Criteria
- Quarterly OKRs for AI program execution

**Engagement Structure:**
- Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4): Stakeholder alignment workshops, current-state documentation
- Phase 2 (Weeks 5–8): Strategy development, architecture design
- Phase 3 (Weeks 9–10): Business case construction, financial modeling
- Phase 4 (Weeks 11–12): Presentation, board alignment, roadmap handoff
- Total: 10–14 weeks

**Ongoing component:** Optional AI Strategy Advisory Retainer post-engagement — see Section 2.9.

**Pricing:** ₹12,00,000 – ₹40,00,000 / $15,000 – $50,000 (scoped by organization size and initiative count)
