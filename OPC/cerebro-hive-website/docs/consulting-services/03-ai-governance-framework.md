# 2.3 AI Governance & Responsible AI Framework

**What it is:** A standalone or embedded consulting service that builds an organization's AI governance structure from the ground up — policies, oversight mechanisms, risk classification systems, ethics committees, audit trails, and compliance frameworks aligned to emerging AI regulations (EU AI Act, DPDP Act India, NIST AI RMF).

**Deliverables:**
- AI Policy Handbook (organizational guidelines, acceptable use, prohibited use)
- AI Risk Classification System — tiered risk model for AI system categorization
- Model Risk Management Framework — model documentation, validation, monitoring standards
- AI Ethics Committee Charter & Operating Procedures
- Data Privacy Impact Assessment Templates for AI systems
- Bias Testing & Fairness Evaluation Protocols
- Incident Response Playbook for AI system failures or misuse
- Regulatory Compliance Matrix — mapped to applicable regulations by geography and industry
- AI Audit Trail Architecture — logging, monitoring, and accountability infrastructure
- Employee AI Acceptable Use Policy
- Vendor AI Due Diligence Questionnaire

**Who needs it:** Any organization deploying AI in customer-facing products, HR processes, financial decisions, or regulated industries (BFSI, Healthcare, Legal, Government).

**Regulatory alignment:** EU AI Act, India's DPDP Act 2023, NIST AI RMF 1.0, ISO/IEC 42001, SEBI guidelines for BFSI AI.
