# 2.5 AI Center of Excellence (CoE) Setup

**What it is:** An end-to-end program to design, build, and operationalize an internal AI Center of Excellence — the organizational unit responsible for owning AI strategy, standards, tooling, and delivery capability inside the client's organization.

**Deliverables:**
- CoE Operating Model — mission, scope, governance, and operating cadence
- Team Structure Blueprint — roles, responsibilities, hiring profiles (AI Product Manager, ML Engineer, Data Scientist, AI Ethicist, AI Trainer, etc.)
- Technology Platform Selection — ML platforms, MLOps tools, data platforms, AI experimentation infrastructure
- Internal AI Standards Library — coding standards, model documentation templates, deployment checklists
- AI Project Intake & Prioritization Process
- Build Capability vs. Buy Vendor Policy
- Budget Framework for AI CoE operations
- 90-Day Launch Plan for the CoE function
- Stakeholder Communication Playbook for CoE adoption

**Engagement structure:** 12–20 week program, with optional embedded advisory for the first 6 months post-launch.

**Target buyer:** Mid-size to enterprise companies that want to own AI capability internally rather than remain perpetually dependent on external vendors.

**Pricing:** ₹20,00,000 – ₹60,00,000 / $25,000 – $80,000
