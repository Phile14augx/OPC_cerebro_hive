# 2.10 Fractional Chief AI Officer (CAiO)

**What it is:** CerebroHive provides a senior AI leader — functioning as the client's Chief AI Officer — on a fractional, part-time basis. The Fractional CAiO owns AI strategy, vendor relationships, team building, governance, and execution oversight without the cost of a full-time executive hire.

**Scope of the role:**
- Define and own the company's AI roadmap
- Chair AI governance committee
- Evaluate and select AI platforms and vendors
- Oversee internal AI team development
- Represent AI strategy to board and investors
- Own AI risk and compliance posture
- Interface between business units and AI teams
- Serve as spokesperson for the organization's AI narrative externally

**Engagement:** 2–4 days per month, minimum 6-month commitment.

**Who needs this:** Scale-ups and mid-market companies that need senior AI leadership but are not ready to hire a full-time CAiO at ₹80L–₹2Cr / $150K–$400K annually.

**Pricing:** ₹3,00,000 – ₹8,00,000 / $4,000 – $10,000 per month
