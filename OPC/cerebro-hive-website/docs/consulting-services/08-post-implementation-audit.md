# 2.8 Post-Implementation AI Audit

**What it is:** A review of AI systems already deployed in a client's operations — examining performance, drift, bias, governance compliance, and business value delivery against original projections.

**Triggers for this engagement:** A deployed AI system underperforming against targets; regulatory or compliance audit requirements; acquisition due diligence; board or investor questions about AI risk.

**Audit dimensions:**
- Model performance degradation (accuracy drift, data distribution shift)
- Business outcome vs. projected ROI
- Governance compliance (against internal policies and external regulations)
- Bias and fairness testing
- Security and adversarial robustness
- Operational resilience (uptime, fallback mechanisms, monitoring coverage)
- Data lineage and documentation completeness

**Output:** AI Audit Report with findings classified by severity (Critical / High / Medium / Low) and a remediation roadmap.
