# 2.1 AI Readiness Assessment

**What it is:** A structured diagnostic engagement that benchmarks an organization's current AI maturity across five dimensions: Data Infrastructure, Process Automation Potential, Workforce AI Literacy, Governance & Risk Posture, and Technology Architecture.

**Deliverables:**
- AI Maturity Scorecard (5-dimension, 25-metric framework)
- Process Inventory Map — identifying top 10–30 automation candidates by ROI potential
- Data Readiness Audit — quality, availability, accessibility, and governance gaps
- AI Risk Surface Assessment — bias exposure, regulatory risk, IP risk, vendor lock-in
- Executive Briefing Deck (board-ready)
- 90-Day Quick-Win Roadmap

**Engagement Structure:**
- Week 1–2: Discovery interviews (C-suite, department heads, IT lead)
- Week 3: Data and system audit
- Week 4: Analysis, scoring, roadmap drafting
- Week 5: Presentation and Q&A sessions
- Total: 4–6 weeks

**Who buys it:** Organizations considering AI adoption for the first time, or those with scattered AI initiatives that haven't produced measurable results.

**Why it matters:** 73% of enterprise AI projects fail to leave the proof-of-concept stage. The Readiness Assessment prevents budget waste by identifying blockers before a single dollar is spent on implementation.

**Pricing:** ₹3,00,000 – ₹8,00,000 / $4,000 – $12,000 (fixed fee)
