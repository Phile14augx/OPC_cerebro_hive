# AI Strategy Pricing Guide

## Overview

Detailed pricing guidance for AI Strategy & Advisory engagements.

## Key Elements

- Advisory Day Rate: $3,000–$5,000 per day for Principal AI Strategist
- Assessment (HiveScore): $25K–$50K fixed price
- Strategy Development: $60K–$120K fixed price
- Roadmap & Business Case: $40K–$80K fixed price
- Executive Workshop: $10K–$25K fixed price
- CoE Setup: $80K–$150K fixed price
- Advisory Retainer: $15K–$30K/month

## Details

AI Strategy & Advisory engagements are priced on a fixed-fee basis for defined-scope projects, ensuring that clients know the total investment upfront and CerebroHive has clear incentive to deliver efficiently. The standard price points are: AI Readiness Assessment (HiveScore™) at $25K-$50K depending on organization size, Executive AI Workshop at $10K-$25K, AI Strategy Development at $75K-$150K, and AI Center of Excellence setup at $100K-$200K. Ongoing strategic advisory retainers are priced at $15K-$30K per month.

CerebroHive's value-based pricing option is available for AI Strategy engagements where a clear financial outcome can be defined upfront. Under this model, the base advisory fee is reduced by 20-30% in exchange for a success fee tied to a defined outcome metric -- for example, a percentage of the first-year cost savings from an AI-driven process automation program. This model aligns CerebroHive's commercial interests directly with client outcomes and is increasingly preferred by CFOs who want to de-risk the AI investment.
