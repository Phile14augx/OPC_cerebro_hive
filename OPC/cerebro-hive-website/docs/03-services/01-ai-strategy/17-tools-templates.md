## Details

CerebroHive's AI Strategy methodology follows a five-phase process: Diagnose, Prioritize, Design, Govern, and Launch. The Diagnose phase uses our HiveScore™ assessment tool to evaluate the organization's current AI maturity across data infrastructure, talent, technology stack, governance, and executive sponsorship. The output is a scored maturity map with specific gaps identified against the baseline required for each use case under consideration.

The Prioritize phase applies the AI Value Canvas™ framework to the full universe of potential AI use cases identified during diagnosis. Each use case is evaluated on four dimensions: business value potential (revenue, cost, risk), implementation complexity (data availability, technical difficulty, organizational change required), time to value (weeks to first measurable outcome), and strategic alignment (fit with corporate priorities and competitive dynamics). The output is a use case portfolio ranked by expected ROI per implementation dollar.

The Design phase translates the prioritized use case portfolio into an implementation roadmap with quarterly objectives, resource requirements, vendor and technology decisions, and go/no-go criteria at each stage. Design also covers the organizational model: whether to build an internal AI Center of Excellence, use a hybrid model with CerebroHive as embedded partner, or run a managed AI operations arrangement. This decision has significant cost and risk implications and is one of the most important outputs of the Strategy phase.

The Govern phase establishes the AI governance framework that will manage risk as the program scales. This includes: AI ethics principles and red-line policies, risk classification methodology aligned to EU AI Act risk tiers, model review and approval workflows, data usage policies, and the escalation framework for high-risk AI decisions. CerebroHive's AI Governance Framework (part of the CerebroSphere™ delivery system) provides a pre-built template that we customize to each client's regulatory environment and risk appetite.

CerebroHive's AI Strategy & Advisory practice is the entry point for most enterprise AI transformations. We bring a structured, evidence-based approach to the most consequential AI decisions executives face: which use cases to fund, which to deprioritize, what the realistic ROI looks like, how to sequence the build, and what organizational changes are required to sustain an AI program beyond the initial project. Our advisory work is grounded in our proprietary HiveScore™ AI Readiness Assessment, which benchmarks an organization against 87 dimensions of AI maturity across data, talent, infrastructure, governance, and culture.
