# 5.5 Labs Partnership Program

**What it is:** A structured engagement where CerebroHive Labs partners with organizations that have proprietary data and a defined AI problem to collaborate on research and build experimental AI solutions.

**Types of partnerships:**
- **Research Partnerships:** Co-produce a research paper or benchmark study; shared IP.
- **Data Partnerships:** Organization provides labeled data; Labs provides AI modeling expertise; outputs shared.
- **Build Partnerships:** Labs builds an experimental prototype in exchange for pilot access and research publication rights.
- **Academic Partnerships:** Joint research with universities and IITs on AI applications in specific domains.

**Benefits to partners:** Early access to research findings, co-branding on publications, discounted commercial access to resulting products, CerebroHive AI Advisory credits.
