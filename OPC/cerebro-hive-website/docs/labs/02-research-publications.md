# 5.2 Research Publications

**What Labs publishes:**

**Annual Report: The State of AI Adoption in India**
- Market research on AI adoption across Indian enterprises by size and industry
- Skills gap analysis — what capabilities organizations need vs. what's available
- Investment trends, ROI data from real deployments, governance maturity benchmark

**Quarterly AI Intelligence Briefing**
- Curated analysis of significant AI developments — new models, capabilities, regulatory changes
- Practical implications for business decision-makers, not just technical coverage
- Distributed to newsletter subscribers and media

**Deep-Dive Technical Papers**
- Architecture papers on AI agent design patterns
- Benchmark comparisons across LLMs for specific business use cases
- Evaluation frameworks for AI governance maturity
- Case study papers documenting (anonymized) client transformation outcomes

**Industry Verticals Research**
- AI in BFSI: Opportunities, risks, and regulatory path
- AI in Healthcare: Clinical AI, administrative automation, regulatory landscape
- AI in Manufacturing: Process optimization, predictive maintenance, quality control
- AI in Legal: Contract intelligence, regulatory compliance, discovery automation
