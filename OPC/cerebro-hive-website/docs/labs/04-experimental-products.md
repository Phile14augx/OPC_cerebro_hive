# 5.4 Experimental Products & Prototypes

**Labs Sandbox:** A portfolio of experimental AI products tested with early users before being promoted to full products or published as open source.

Current sandbox projects:

**NegotiationOS** — An AI system trained on negotiation psychology and business contracts that coaches users in real time during commercial negotiations via an earpiece or sidebar interface.

**PolicyBot** — An AI agent that reads a company's internal policy documents and answers employee questions with citations — eliminating the "email HR for everything" problem.

**ContractLens** — A document intelligence product for contract review — extracts key terms, flags unusual clauses, compares to standard positions, and generates a risk summary in under 60 seconds.

**MeetingIntel** — A meeting intelligence system that goes beyond transcription — identifies action items, decisions, commitments, risks raised, and sentiment — and automatically routes outputs to the relevant systems (CRM, project tracker, email).

**TalentRadar** — An AI talent analytics system that predicts attrition risk, identifies flight-risk employees early, and surfaces career development recommendations — built for HR leadership.
