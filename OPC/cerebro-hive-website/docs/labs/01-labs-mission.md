# 5.1 Mission & Scope

CerebroHive Labs is the R&D and innovation arm of the organization. It serves four functions:

1. **IP Generation:** Building proprietary frameworks, methodologies, and tools that power the other three divisions and create competitive moat.
2. **Thought Leadership:** Publishing research that establishes CerebroHive as an authority in AI automation and transformation.
3. **Open Source:** Contributing tools to the developer community to build brand recognition among technical audiences.
4. **Product Incubation:** Prototyping and validating AI product ideas before committing to full product development.
