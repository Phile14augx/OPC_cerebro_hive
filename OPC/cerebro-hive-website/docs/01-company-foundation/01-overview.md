# CerebroHive -- Company Overview

CerebroHive is an AI-native enterprise engineering and transformation partner founded in 2026. We exist at the intersection of executive strategy, deep AI engineering, and proprietary delivery frameworks -- combining capabilities that most firms offer separately into a single, integrated practice.

Unlike traditional software consultancies that bolt AI onto existing development offerings, or strategy firms that advise without building, CerebroHive was designed from the ground up to deliver measurable business outcomes through AI. Every engagement -- from a two-week AI readiness assessment to a multi-year managed AI operations retainer -- is anchored to quantifiable ROI, not technology for its own sake.

## What Makes CerebroHive Different

- **AI-Native Engineering DNA** -- Every practice and every engineer at CerebroHive has AI at the center, not the periphery. We do not repurpose general software development teams for AI work.
- **Proprietary Frameworks** -- We bring 10 battle-tested delivery frameworks that systematize best practices, reduce project risk, and accelerate time-to-value.
- **End-to-End Capability** -- From executive AI strategy through data engineering, application development, MLOps, and managed operations -- we do not hand off mid-engagement.
- **Outcome-Based Commercial Models** -- We offer fixed-price, time-and-materials, retainer, and outcome-based contracts aligned to how clients want to buy.
- **Quantiva ERP** -- Our own AI-embedded ERP product serves as proof of engineering capability and an additional revenue stream.

## Strategic Intent

CerebroHive's strategic intent is to become the definitive AI-native enterprise transformation partner for mid-market and enterprise organizations across North America, the UK, Australia, and the Middle East by 2030. We will achieve this by combining advisory depth with engineering excellence, proprietary IP that scales beyond billable hours, and a relentless focus on measurable client outcomes.

The global enterprise AI consulting market is estimated at $45B in 2026 and growing at 32% CAGR, driven by accelerating enterprise AI adoption following the generative AI inflection point of 2023-2024. CerebroHive is positioned to capture a disproportionate share of this growth by targeting the segment most underserved by current market participants: mid-market enterprises ($100M-$2B revenue) that need full-stack AI capability but cannot access it from the large system integrators (minimum engagement sizes are too large) or from boutique specialists (insufficient breadth).

Enterprise AI adoption is moving from pilot to production at scale. In 2026, 74% of Fortune 500 companies report having AI initiatives underway, but only 19% report AI deployed in core business processes at scale. This gap between initiative count and production deployment is CerebroHive's primary opportunity -- the firm specializes precisely in closing that gap through engineering excellence and operational rigor.

The competitive landscape for enterprise AI consulting is fragmented between large system integrators (Accenture, Deloitte, IBM), hyperscaler professional services arms (AWS, Azure, Google), pure-play AI boutiques, and traditional management consultancies expanding into AI. CerebroHive's differentiation lies in the combination of engineering depth that boutiques offer, the broad service portfolio that large SIs offer, and proprietary IP that none of them possess at comparable depth.
