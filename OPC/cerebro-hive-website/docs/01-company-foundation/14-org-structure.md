# Organizational Structure

CerebroHive is organized around practice areas that mirror our service lines, supported by a shared delivery and operations function. The structure is designed to be flat, fast, and client-centric.

## Organizational Design Principles

- Flat hierarchy -- maximum 3 layers from individual contributor to founder
- Senior people do client work -- no 'management-only' roles until $10M ARR
- Practice leads own P&L for their service line
- Shared delivery infrastructure (QA, PMO, security) serves all practices
- Knowledge sharing is explicit in operating model -- weekly cross-practice sessions

## Org Chart (Founding Team → Year 2)

```text
Founder / CEO
├── Practice Lead: AI Strategy & Advisory
│   └── AI Strategists (2–3)
├── Practice Lead: AI Engineering (GenAI, Agents, MLOps)
│   └── AI/ML Engineers (4–6)
├── Practice Lead: Data & Platform
│   └── Data Engineers (2–3)
├── Practice Lead: Enterprise Software (Quantiva)
│   └── Full-Stack Engineers (3–4)
├── Head of Sales & GTM
│   └── Account Executives (1–2)
├── Head of Operations (PMO, QA, Finance)
└── Head of Marketing (Content, SEO, Demand Gen)
```

CerebroHive's knowledge management system ensures that every engagement generates reusable IP: framework refinements, code templates, case study data, and benchmark metrics flow into a shared repository that is used to improve the next engagement. This flywheel effect means CerebroHive gets measurably better at each service type as the engagement count increases.

CerebroHive is organized into four internal practices -- AI Strategy, AI Engineering, AI Operations, and AI Products -- each led by a Practice Director who reports to the CEO. Each practice has dedicated sales support (solution architects), delivery leadership (principal consultants and engagement managers), and technical depth (senior engineers and data scientists). This structure ensures that the firm can staff any engagement with the right expertise without creating internal competition for shared resources.

The firm's talent model prioritizes hiring experienced practitioners with 5+ years of hands-on AI/ML production experience over candidates with academic credentials alone. CerebroHive runs a rigorous 4-week onboarding program that covers the firm's delivery frameworks, commercial model, and quality standards before any new hire is client-facing. Senior hires go through a trial engagement model where they shadow a Principal for their first client interaction.
