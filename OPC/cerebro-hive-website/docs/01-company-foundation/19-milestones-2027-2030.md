CerebroHive's 2026 milestones are organized around four pillars: foundation-setting, first client delivery, IP development, and market visibility. Foundation milestones include: completing the core hiring plan (8 senior practitioners by Q2), finalizing the CerebroSphere™ and HiveMatrix™ framework documentation, launching the CerebroHive website and thought leadership program, and completing the Quantiva ERP MVP for pilot deployment.

First client delivery milestones for 2026: close and complete 4 AI Readiness Assessment engagements by end of Q2 (generating $200K and producing 4 case study narratives), convert 2 assessments to full AI Strategy engagements by Q3, and achieve first Quantiva ERP pilot deployment at a mid-market manufacturing client by Q4. Client satisfaction target: 9+ NPS on all 2026 engagements.

The 2027-2030 roadmap targets: 10 named accounts under active engagement by end of 2027, Quantiva ERP achieving SOC 2 Type II certification and 20 production deployments by mid-2028, the CerebroHive State of Enterprise AI annual report establishing itself as a market reference document by 2027 release, and international office openings in London and Dubai by Q1 2028.
