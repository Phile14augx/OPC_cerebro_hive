# 7.6 AI Solutions for Retail & E-Commerce

- **Product recommendation engines:** Personalized recommendations based on browsing, purchase, and behavioral data
- **Inventory optimization:** Demand forecasting, automated reorder, supplier communication
- **Customer support automation:** Order status, returns, refunds, product questions handled by AI agents
- **Dynamic pricing:** Real-time pricing optimization based on demand, inventory, and competitor signals
- **Content generation at scale:** Product descriptions, category pages, SEO content for large catalogs
- **Fraud detection:** Real-time order fraud scoring, payment fraud detection
- **Loyalty program automation:** Points management, personalized offers, win-back campaigns
