# 4.5 AI Product Management

**What it is:** A specialized program for product managers, product owners, and aspiring PMs who want to build AI-native products — covering the distinct skills needed when the product contains AI as a core component.

**Curriculum (6 weeks, live cohort):**

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | AI Product Fundamentals — how AI products differ from traditional software |
| 2 | Defining AI Requirements — probabilistic outputs, confidence thresholds, edge cases |
| 3 | AI UX Design — designing for uncertainty, transparency, and trust |
| 4 | AI Ethics in Product — bias, fairness, explainability, consent |
| 5 | Building & Evaluating AI Products — model selection, evaluation metrics, A/B testing |
| 6 | Roadmapping AI Products — technical debt, model versioning, dataset management |

**Outcome:** CerebroHive AI Product Manager Certificate
