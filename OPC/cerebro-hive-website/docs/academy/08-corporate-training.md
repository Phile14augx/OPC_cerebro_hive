# 4.8 Corporate AI Training Programs

**What it is:** Custom-designed AI training programs for organizations — delivered to their teams, aligned to their tools, and built around their specific use cases. Not generic courses repurposed for enterprise; genuine custom curriculum.

**Engagement process:**
1. Training Needs Analysis — identify skill gaps, current AI adoption level, target outcomes
2. Curriculum Design — custom modules built around the client's tools, processes, and industry
3. Delivery Planning — format selection (live virtual, in-person workshop, self-paced, blended)
4. Content Creation — custom exercises, case studies using the client's actual processes
5. Delivery — by CerebroHive instructors or train-the-trainer for large organizations
6. Assessment & Certification — client-branded certificates, competency assessments
7. Impact Measurement — pre/post skill assessments, manager surveys, usage tracking

**Standard corporate training packages:**
