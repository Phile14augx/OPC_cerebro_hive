# 4.9 CerebroHive Academy Certification System

**Overview:** CerebroHive operates a structured certification system with three tiers:

**Tier 1 — Foundation Certificates**
- AI Foundations Certificate
- Prompt Engineering Certificate
- AI Tools Practitioner Certificate

**Tier 2 — Practitioner Certificates**
- AI Automation Practitioner Certificate
- AI Marketing Practitioner Certificate
- AI Finance Practitioner Certificate
- AI Product Manager Certificate
- AI Data Analyst Certificate

**Tier 3 — Advanced / Specialist Certificates**
- AI Agent Developer Certificate
- AI Transformation Specialist Certificate
- AI Ethics & Governance Certificate
- CerebroHive Certified AI Leader

**Certificate features:**
- Digital badge (Credly-compatible) for LinkedIn profile
- Verifiable credential with unique certificate ID
- Portfolio artifact (project submission) for Practitioner and above
- Alumni community access
- Annual recertification option as AI capabilities evolve
