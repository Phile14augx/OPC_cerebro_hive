# 4.3 AI for Business Leaders (Executive Program)

**What it is:** A high-density, executive-level program designed for C-suite executives, VPs, and senior directors who need strategic AI fluency — not technical depth, but enough understanding to make sound AI investment decisions, evaluate vendors, lead AI governance, and communicate AI strategy credibly.

**Format:** Live virtual cohort (8 sessions × 90 minutes over 4 weeks) + async coursework + executive peer community

**Curriculum:**

| Session | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | The AI Opportunity: Strategic framing for executive decision-makers |
| 2 | AI Investment Decisions: What to fund, what to buy, what to build |
| 3 | AI Governance & Risk: What every executive is liable for |
| 4 | Building vs. Buying AI: Frameworks for technology decisions |
| 5 | Leading AI Transformation: Change management and organizational readiness |
| 6 | AI in Your P&L: Cost models, ROI calculation, and financial impact |
| 7 | The Future of AI: 3-year horizon briefing — what's coming and what it means |
| 8 | Personal Action Planning: Your AI leadership agenda for the next 90 days |

**Guest sessions:** Industry leaders from AI-native companies share real transformation stories (2–3 guest sessions per cohort).

**Outcome:** CerebroHive AI Leadership Certificate; peer network of senior executives across cohort

**Pricing:** ₹45,000 / $600 per executive; corporate group rate: ₹3,00,000 for up to 10 participants
