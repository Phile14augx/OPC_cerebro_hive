# 4.10 AI Bootcamp — Intensive Format

**What it is:** High-intensity, short-duration bootcamps for professionals who need to develop AI skills fast — delivered as immersive weekend or week-long programs.

**Formats:**
- **Weekend Intensive (2 days, in-person):** 16 hours, focused on one specific AI skill area
- **Week-Long Sprint (5 days, hybrid):** 40 hours, comprehensive skill development with live project
- **4-Week Cohort (part-time, evenings):** 32 hours across 4 weeks, career-focused AI transition

**Bootcamp topics available:**
- AI Automation Bootcamp (most popular)
- AI Agent Development Bootcamp (developer-focused)
- AI Strategy Bootcamp (business-focused)
- Generative AI & Prompt Engineering Bootcamp
- AI for Healthcare Professionals
- AI for Legal Professionals
