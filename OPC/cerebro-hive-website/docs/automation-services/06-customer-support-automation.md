# 3.6 Customer Support Automation

**What it is:** End-to-end automation of the customer support function — from first contact through resolution — using AI agents, intelligent routing, and automated resolution systems.

**Components:**

**Tier 0 — Self-Service AI:**
- AI chatbot on website, app, or WhatsApp that handles the top 50–70% of support queries without human involvement
- Knowledge base integration — bot answers from product documentation, FAQs, and past resolved tickets
- Multilingual support (English, Hindi, Tamil, and other regional languages for India-market clients)
- Escalation logic — recognizes when a human is needed and hands off with full context

**Tier 1 — Agent Assist AI:**
- Real-time suggestions to human agents during live chat or call
- Automatic ticket summarization when a human picks up an escalated ticket
- Suggested reply generation — agent approves and sends with one click
- Knowledge article recommendation during ticket resolution
- Sentiment detection — alerts supervisors when a customer is escalating emotionally

**Tier 2 — Back-Office Automation:**
- Automated ticket classification and routing
- SLA monitoring and escalation workflows
- Repetitive resolution automation (order status, password resets, refund initiation within policy)
- Post-resolution survey automation and feedback categorization
- Weekly support insights report generation (volume, CSAT, resolution time, top issue categories)

**Platforms we integrate with:** Zendesk, Freshdesk, Intercom, Salesforce Service Cloud, Zoho Desk, WhatsApp Business API, Twilio.
