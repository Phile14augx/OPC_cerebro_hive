# 3.4 CRM Automation & Optimization

**What it is:** Deep configuration, customization, and AI augmentation of CRM platforms — turning under-utilized CRM installations into intelligence-driven revenue operations systems.

**Platforms:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Pipedrive, Monday CRM, Freshsales.

**What we build:**

- **Lead scoring models** — AI-powered scoring based on behavioral signals, firmographic data, and historical conversion patterns
- **Pipeline automation** — stage advancement triggers, deal health scoring, inactivity alerts, automated follow-up sequences
- **Contact enrichment** — automated enrichment from LinkedIn, Clearbit, Apollo, and public data sources
- **Sales playbook automation** — trigger-based content delivery, talk track prompts, competitive battlecards surfaced at the right deal stage
- **Revenue forecasting** — AI-assisted forecasting models integrated into CRM dashboards
- **Customer health scoring** — churn risk models surfaced within the CRM for account managers
- **Marketing-to-sales handoff automation** — MQL scoring, routing, SLA monitoring, and feedback loops
- **CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) automation** — proposal generation, approval workflows, discount guardrails
- **Customer 360 view** — unified customer record pulling data from support, billing, product usage, and communication history
