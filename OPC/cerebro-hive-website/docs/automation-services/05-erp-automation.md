# 3.5 ERP Automation & AI Integration

**What it is:** Automation of complex ERP workflows and integration of AI capabilities into ERP systems — reducing manual data entry, accelerating financial close, improving procurement accuracy, and generating intelligent alerts.

**ERP platforms:** SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Tally, Odoo, NetSuite, ERPNext.

**AI & automation layers we add:**

- **Intelligent document processing (IDP):** Extract data from invoices, purchase orders, receipts, and contracts using OCR + LLMs — eliminate manual data entry into ERP
- **Automated 3-way matching:** PO, GRN, and invoice matching with exception flagging and routing
- **Predictive inventory management:** Demand forecasting models that update reorder points and safety stock recommendations automatically
- **Financial close automation:** Month-end journal entry suggestions, reconciliation automation, intercompany matching
- **AI-powered spend analytics:** Categorization, supplier consolidation opportunities, maverick spend detection
- **Cash flow forecasting:** ML models that predict cash position 30/60/90 days out based on AR, AP, and historical patterns
- **Compliance monitoring:** Real-time flagging of transactions that deviate from policy or trigger regulatory thresholds
