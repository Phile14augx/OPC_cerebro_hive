# 3.8 Document Intelligence & Processing

**What it is:** An AI-powered document processing system that ingests unstructured documents in any format (PDF, Word, Excel, email, images, scanned paper) and extracts, classifies, validates, and routes data — eliminating manual document handling.

**Document types handled:**
- Invoices and receipts
- Contracts and agreements
- Identity documents (KYC — Aadhaar, PAN, passport, driving licence)
- Bank statements
- Medical reports and prescriptions
- Legal filings and court documents
- Insurance policies and claims forms
- Academic certificates and transcripts

**Capabilities:**
- **Extraction:** Pull specific fields from documents with high accuracy (>95% for standard formats)
- **Classification:** Route documents to the correct workflow based on type, content, or sender
- **Validation:** Cross-check extracted data against databases, business rules, or other documents
- **Summarization:** Generate natural-language summaries of long documents (contracts, reports, filings)
- **Comparison:** Compare two document versions and highlight differences (contract redlines, policy changes)
- **Q&A over documents:** Allow users to ask natural-language questions about document contents

**Integration points:** Email inboxes, cloud storage (Google Drive, SharePoint, S3), ERP systems, CRM, HRMS.

**Pricing:** ₹3,00,000 – ₹18,00,000 / $4,000 – $22,000 for implementation; usage-based pricing available for high-volume operations
