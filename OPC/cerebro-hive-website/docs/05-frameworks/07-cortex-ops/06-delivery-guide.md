## Practitioner Delivery Guide -- Detail

The CortexOps™ dashboard is the operational nerve center for CerebroHive-managed AI programs: a real-time view of all production models' performance metrics, data pipeline health, inference endpoint availability, cost trends, and open incidents. The dashboard integrates with PagerDuty for incident alerting, Grafana for metric visualization, and the client's existing ITSM system for ticket management. Monthly CortexOps™ performance reports provide executive-level visibility into AI system health and business value delivered.

CortexOps™ is CerebroHive's AI operations framework -- the systematic methodology for running, monitoring, maintaining, and continuously improving production AI systems. The framework covers model performance monitoring (accuracy, latency, throughput), data drift detection (input distribution shift, feature drift, concept drift), incident response (detection, diagnosis, response, and post-mortem), retraining automation (trigger design, pipeline execution, validation, and promotion), cost management (inference cost monitoring, optimization, and capacity planning), and AI system health reporting.

CortexOps™ was built in response to a consistent pattern CerebroHive observed in enterprise AI programs: organizations invest heavily in building and deploying AI systems, then discover that production operations require a completely different skill set and process discipline than development. Model performance degrades silently. Data pipelines fail without alerting the AI team. Inference costs spike unexpectedly. CortexOps™ eliminates these operational blind spots by establishing explicit monitoring, alerting, and response workflows from the first day of production deployment.
