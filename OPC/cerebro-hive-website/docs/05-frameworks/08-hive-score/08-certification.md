## Practitioner Certification -- Detail

HiveScore™ is CerebroHive's AI Readiness Assessment tool -- a structured diagnostic that evaluates an organization's current capability to develop, deploy, and operate AI systems at enterprise scale. The assessment covers 87 dimensions across 6 domains: Data Maturity (data availability, quality, governance, and infrastructure), Technical Infrastructure (cloud platform, compute, integration, and security), Talent & Organization (AI skills, org structure, and AI culture), Governance & Risk (ethics policy, regulatory compliance, and risk management), Executive Alignment (AI vision, sponsorship, and investment commitment), and AI Program Maturity (existing AI initiatives, delivery capability, and vendor relationships).

HiveScore™ produces a scored maturity profile across all 87 dimensions, benchmarked against CerebroHive's industry-specific baseline data from 100+ enterprise AI programs. The benchmark comparison surfaces the specific gaps between the client's current state and the capability level required to execute the AI use cases under consideration. This gap analysis is the foundation of the AI implementation roadmap: it defines what enabler investments are required before each use case can be successfully deployed.

The HiveScore™ assessment process takes 4-6 weeks for a mid-size enterprise (5,000-50,000 employees) and involves structured interviews with 15-25 stakeholders across IT, data, AI/ML, business operations, risk, compliance, and executive leadership. The interview protocol is supplemented by a technical environment review (cloud architecture, data platform, existing AI systems), a data landscape survey (primary data sources, quality metrics, governance status), and a talent assessment (skills inventory and organizational capability mapping).
