## Framework in Action -- Case Study -- Detail

DecisionDNA™ is CerebroHive's framework for AI-assisted enterprise decision intelligence -- the methodology for embedding AI into the decision-making processes that drive the most business value. Unlike generic AI strategy frameworks, DecisionDNA™ starts with decisions (Which supplier should we select? Which customer is likely to churn? Which claims require manual review?) rather than with data or models, and works backward to define the AI system required to improve each decision's speed, accuracy, or consistency.

DecisionDNA™ maps enterprise decisions across three dimensions: Decision Frequency (how often the decision is made per day/week/month), Decision Value (the revenue, cost, or risk impact of each individual decision), and Decision Improvability (the degree to which AI can improve decision quality, measured as the gap between current decision accuracy and the accuracy achievable with AI augmentation). This mapping identifies the highest-ROI decision improvement opportunities -- typically, high-frequency decisions with high aggregate value and a clear data trail that makes AI training feasible.

The DecisionDNA™ implementation methodology follows four steps: Identify (map the decision landscape and score on frequency, value, and improvability), Design (specify the AI model type, required data inputs, output format, and human-in-the-loop requirements for the top-priority decisions), Build (develop and validate the AI decision support system against historical decision outcomes), and Embed (integrate the AI decision support into existing workflows with appropriate governance controls and performance monitoring).
