## IP Ownership & Licensing -- Detail

The AI Value Canvas™ is available as a digital tool integrated into CerebroHive's client portal, allowing engagement teams and client stakeholders to collaboratively populate, review, and approve use case evaluations. The digital canvas feeds directly into the HiveMatrix™ portfolio prioritization tool, populating the impact and feasibility scores that drive the portfolio allocation recommendation. Completed canvases are stored in the CerebroHive knowledge base and used to improve the accuracy of value estimates for similar use cases in future engagements.

The AI Value Canvas™ is CerebroHive's use case evaluation and business case development tool -- a structured one-page framework that captures everything a decision-maker needs to evaluate an AI investment: the problem being solved, the AI approach, the data required, the expected value created, the implementation cost, the risks, and the success metrics. The canvas is used in every CerebroHive strategic advisory engagement and has been applied to evaluate 500+ enterprise AI use cases.

The AI Value Canvas™ consists of nine sections: Business Problem (the specific decision, process, or outcome to be improved), AI Solution (the model type, approach, and technical design at a summary level), Data Requirements (the data sources, volumes, quality requirements, and governance considerations), Value Creation (quantified revenue, cost, and risk impact with assumptions stated explicitly), Implementation Investment (consulting, technology, internal resource, and ongoing operational cost), Time to Value (weeks to first measurable outcome and months to full value capture), Key Risks (technical, organizational, regulatory, and data risks with mitigation approaches), Success Metrics (the specific KPIs that will be tracked to evaluate the AI system's business impact), and Dependencies (the data, technology, and organizational enablers that must be in place before implementation can succeed).
